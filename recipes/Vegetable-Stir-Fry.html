Vegetable Stir-Fry

Description:

A quick and healthy dish packed with colorful vegetables sautéed in a savory sauce.

Ingredients:

  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 1 bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 carrot, julienned
  • 1 zucchini, sliced
  • 1 cup broccoli florets
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon oyster sauce (optional)
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil
  • Cooked rice or noodles (for serving)

Steps:

  1. Heat vegetable oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat.
  2. Add garlic and cook for 30 seconds.
  3. Add all vegetables and stir-fry for 5–7 minutes until tender-crisp.
  4. Stir in soy sauce, oyster sauce (if using), and sesame oil.
  5. Cook for another 1–2 minutes to coat the vegetables.
  6. Serve hot over cooked rice or noodles.
