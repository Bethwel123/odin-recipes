Chicken Recipe

Description:

This classic roasted chicken is juicy on the inside, crispy on the outside and full of flavor.

Seasoned with a blend of herbs, garlic and lemon, it's a perfect centerpiece for a dinner

Ingredients:

  • 1 whole chicken
  • 2 teaspoons olive oil
  • 1 lemon, halved
  • 1 onion, Quarted(optional for roasting in the pan)
  • 4-5 gralic cloves, smarshed or minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried rosemary
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • Vegetables like carrots and potatoes, optional for roasting with the chicken

Steps:

  1. Prehear the oven to 425°F (220°C)
  2. Prepare the chicken
    • Remove the giblets from the cavity
    • Pat the chicken thoroughly with paper towels(especially the skin)
  3. Make the seasoning mix
    • In a small bowl, mix together:
      • 2 tablespoons olive oil
      • 1 teaspoon salt
      • ½ teaspoon black pepper
      • 1 teaspoon paprika (optional)
      • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
      • 1 teaspoon dried rosemary
      • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
      • 4-5 garlic cloves, minced or smashed
