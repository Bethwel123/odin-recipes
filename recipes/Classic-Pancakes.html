Classic Pancakes

Description:

Light, fluffy pancakes made from scratch. Perfect for breakfast or brunch.

Ingredients:

  • 1½ cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • 1¼ cups milk
  • 1 egg
  • 3 tablespoons melted butter
  • Butter or oil for the pan
  • Maple syrup, fruits, or toppings (optional)

Steps:

  1. In a bowl, whisk together flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt.
  2. In another bowl, mix milk, egg, and melted butter.
  3. Combine wet and dry ingredients until just mixed (do not overmix).
  4. Heat a lightly oiled pan or griddle over medium heat.
  5. Pour ¼ cup of batter for each pancake.
  6. Cook until bubbles form on the surface, then flip and cook until golden brown.
  7. Serve warm with your favorite toppings.
